This is a Fire Emblem 7 / Fire Emblem 6 fangame structured after Fire Emblem 4's gameplay mechanics, developed by a group of volunteers and made available to play free of charge.

To contribute you will need the latest version of Lex Talionis Engine (Python version) and ideally something like Github Desktop for easily pushing your changed, and pulling the latest changes.
